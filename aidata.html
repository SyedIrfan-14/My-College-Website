AI Data Battle Royale

Each players will get an problem statement with required data sets, They have to build an machine learning models using statistical techniques and programming methodology, and they have to visualize the output and to relatively or closes to solve wins.

Rules And Regulations:-

1)Indivduals Should participate no teams

2)Each one will get a problem statement

3)Using Statistical tools and build their model to get desired output

4)Each Will get Datasets with their respective problem statement

5)Time limit to the Battle is 30 mins

6)Should not use AI like chatgpt, etc..

7)They can use as much tools that are not operated by AI

8)After Time the players should demonstrate and explain the results that they got from the model

9)Based on the output and the algorithm they used to sort the winners

Date: 22nd september

Venue: SRM Vadapalani Auditorium

Time: 9:30 AM - 12:30 PM
