AI Websculpt

Join us for an exhilarating blend of artificial intelligence and human creativity in the "AI Websculpt " Participants will be tasked with designing a website using Canva, with some unique twists to test your innovation and adaptability:

This event is not just about web design, but also about exploring the synergies between AI and human creativity. So, roll up your sleeves, ignite your imaginative sparks, and let's craft digital magic together!

Competition Guidelines:

1. Topic: A designated topic will be provided.

2. Rounds: There will be one round.

3. Time Limit: Participants will have 2 hours to create their own website using Canva.

4. Content Sources: - Images: All images must be obtained from AI-generated platforms. - Content Mix: Your website's content should consist of a 50-50 balance, with 50% generated by AI tools and 50% from your own creative ideas.

5. Judging: The judges will evaluate the websites and announce the winner.

Venue: SRM Vadapalani Auditorium

Time: 9:30 AM - 12:30 PM
