AI Treasure Hunt

An "AI Treasure Hunt" could refer to a game or competition that involves the use of artificial intelligence (AI) techniques to solve challenges or puzzles in order to locate hidden treasures or rewards. This concept combines elements of AI, problem-solving, and exploration.

Rules

1. It’s a team event with 3 members in each team.

2. There are two rounds in this game - a. 9.00 to 12.00 (1st round) b. 12.30 to 3.30 (2nd round)

3. Teams will be given a system each to start with.

4. We won’t be accepting any entries after 11.00.

5. The teams can enter the game at any point of time before 11.00 but they will get a maximum of 90 mins to solve the round.

6. Irrespective of when the team starts they will be timed accordingly and the recorded time will be noted.

7. The top 8 teams can proceed to the second round.

8. Qualified teams will again get 90 mins to solve the second round.

9. The top teams with the least time taken to solve the 2nd round will be the winners.

10. All external devices, google, internet, etc are allowed.

Date: 22nd september

Venue: SRM Vadapalani Auditorium

Time: 9:30 AM - 12:30 PM
