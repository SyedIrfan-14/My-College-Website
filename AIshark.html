AI Shark Tank

AI Shark Tank is an exciting event where participants showcase their entrepreneurial skills by presenting innovative business ideas that leverage artificial intelligence (AI) technologies.

Event Details:

1. Teams of 3-5 members will participate in this competition.

2. Participants will use AI tools of their choice to develop their business ideas and presentations.

3. The competition will consist of multiple rounds, the number of which will depend on the total number of participants.

4. Each team will have to deliver a presentation that is a minimum of 5 minutes long, followed by a question and answer session with the judges.

AI Tools to Be Used:

Participants are free to use any AI tools they want to develop their business ideas and presentations.

Some popular AI tools include:

Namelix for business naming, Canva for design, ChatGPT/Google Bard for copywriting, Grammarly for editing, Figma for prototyping

Event Structure:

Round 1: 1.) Participants will have 7-10 days to prepare their business ideas and presentations. 2.) On-spot registration is allowed but offers limited additional time. 3.) Human judges will evaluate the presentations in this round.

Round 2: 1.) Participants will receive an AI prompt 30-45 minutes before the round starts. 2.) Teams can choose to present using either a chalkboard or a PowerPoint presentation. 3.) Evaluation in this round will be conducted by both human judges and AI.

Rules and Guidelines

1. Participants must maintain a respectful and professional demeanor throughout the event.

2. Vulgarity is strictly prohibited.

3. Presentation duration must be between 5 and 10 minutes.

Date: 22nd september

Venue: SRM Vadapalani Auditorium

Time: 9:30 AM - 12:30 PM
