AI Quiz

Join us for a fun and engaging AI quiz , a thrilling competition that will challenge your understanding of AI concepts , technologies and applications. This event is open to all enthusiasts who want to expand their AI horizons.

Rules and Regulations

1) The quiz will be conducted in single round.

2) 15 questions will be asked in the quiz

3) Each question will be displayed for 30 seconds.

4) A winner and a runner will be selected.

(Note : In case of more number of participants participating the quiz. We will be conducting it in 2 rounds. The total number of participants will be separated into 2 batches. For each batch the quiz will be conducted. From each batch some no. of participants will be selected according to the no. of right answers and sent to the round 2 where they will be conducted a quiz and finalized with a winner and a runner.)

Date: 22nd september

Venue: SRM Vadapalani Auditorium

Time: 9:30 AM - 12:30 PM
